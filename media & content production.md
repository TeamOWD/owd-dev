## **What Actually Goes Into Production**

A single piece of content moves through a full production workflow:

**Strategy** — We define what the piece needs to accomplish and where it fits in your marketing.

**Scripting** — Every video is scripted with intention. The words and structure matter.

**Your Review** — You know your business and voice best. The script goes to you for edits before anything is filmed.

**Filming** — We direct you on how to film — framing, lighting, wardrobe, background. Most clients film and send us raw footage. On-site production is available when it makes sense.

**Edit Briefing** — We build a full project review for our editors: pacing, cuts, graphics, music, captions, and the platform it's built for.

**Editing & Quality Control** — Nothing leaves our team without passing QC.

**Your Approval** — It comes back to you for review and revisions until it's right.

**Optimization & Syndication** — We optimize each piece for its primary platform — Instagram or YouTube — and syndicate to the other platforms that make sense.

This is not a quick task. It's a real production process, and we take it seriously.
