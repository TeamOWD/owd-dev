Straight Answers About Local SEO

Unlike paid advertising that delivers instant results, Local SEO is a long-term strategy. Most clients see initial rankings improve within 3–4 months, with significant lead generation starting around month 6. By month 12, you should be firmly established in your local market with consistent, predictable lead flow — at a fraction of what you were spending on ads.

Your Google Business Profile can only take you so far. When homeowners click through from Maps to your website and find a slow, confusing, or outdated page, they leave — and your Local SEO investment is wasted. More importantly, service area pages, proper schema markup, NAP consistency, and page speed are all direct Local SEO ranking signals. A poorly built site actively undermines your Map Pack rankings. The best Local SEO results come when your website and local presence are built as one integrated system.

After full optimization (12+ months), our successful clients typically receive 25–50 qualified local leads per month at $15–30 per lead. Results depend on your service area size and population, local competition level, your current online presence, and the mix of services you offer. During your free market analysis, we'll give you a realistic projection specific to your market — not a number we think you want to hear.

No ethical agency can guarantee specific rankings — Google's algorithm is outside anyone's control, and anyone promising a specific Map Pack position is not being straight with you. What we do guarantee is our process, our dedicated team's commitment to your market, and complete transparency in reporting. You'll know exactly what we're doing, why, and what results it's generating. The reason most of our clients stay with us long-term is simple: the numbers speak for themselves.
