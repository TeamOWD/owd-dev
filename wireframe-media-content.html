Media & Content Production

Strategic video and content built to generate leads — not just fill a feed.

The Approach

This Isn't Social Media. It's Search Infrastructure.

At Outwork'em Digital, our Media & Content Production division handles strategic video and podcast content for home service businesses that are serious about growth.

Here's what most people get wrong: they think "content" means pulling out an iPhone, hitting record, and posting it somewhere. That's not what we do.

Every piece of content we produce has a job — to answer the questions your customers are already asking, build your authority, and connect you with the people who are ready to hire you. Google now indexes social media, which means the videos we make aren't just sitting on your feed. They're working for your search visibility around the clock.

This is content as infrastructure. Built with strategy. Backed by a process.

Our Framework

Rooted in a Proven Framework

Our approach is built on the philosophy from They Ask You Answer by Marcus Sheridan: your customers are typing their questions into Google right now. The business that shows up and answers those questions — on video, in blogs, across platforms — becomes the trusted authority before the phone ever rings.

That's the difference between content that entertains and content that converts.

We make the second kind.

The Process

What Actually Goes Into Production

A single piece of content moves through a full production workflow.

1

Strategy

We define what the piece needs to accomplish and where it fits in your marketing.

2

Scripting

Every video is scripted with intention. The words and structure matter.

3

Your Review

You know your business and voice best. The script goes to you for edits before anything is filmed.

4

Filming

We direct you on how to film — framing, lighting, wardrobe, background. Most clients film and send raw footage. On-site production is available when it makes sense.

5

Edit Briefing

We build a full project review for our editors: pacing, cuts, graphics, music, captions, and the platform it's built for.

6

Editing & Quality Control

Nothing leaves our team without passing QC.

7

Your Approval

It comes back to you for review and revisions until it's right.

8

Optimization & Syndication

We optimize each piece for its primary platform — Instagram or YouTube — and syndicate to the other platforms that make sense.

This is not a quick task. It's a real production process, and we take it seriously.

What We Are — And What We Are Not

What We Are

A strategic media partner.

Strategic video and podcast content built to generate leads, build authority, and work for your search visibility — backed by a real production process.

What We Are Not

A social media management agency.

Posting every day, managing your comments and inbox, engaging your audience, chasing trends — that's a full-time job that requires a dedicated person. The same goes for a full-time content creator, creative director, or DM setter.

There are plenty of options out there if you just want to check a box. But if you want this done well, it takes a dedicated person — at minimum part-time — behind it. We're honest about where our lane begins and ends.

How We Work Together

If Raw Video Doesn't Come In, Content Doesn't Go Out.

Our process requires participation from both sides. We handle everything on the production end — but we need footage to work with.

The clients who get the most out of working with us take the filming seriously, follow our direction, and deliver assets on time. When that happens, we do exceptional work together.
