Five Touchpoints That Protect and Grow Your Revenue

Every contractor loses clients silently. The PRRRR Method fixes the five stages where revenue leaks — and turns them into growth opportunities.

R

Make your best clients feel seen and appreciated after every job. Higher goodwill means more repeat calls and stronger word-of-mouth.
